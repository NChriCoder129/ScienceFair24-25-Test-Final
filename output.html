1. Pesto Pasta Caprese Salad

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Yield: 6 servings

Nutritional Facts:

calories: 169 kcal
carbohydrateContent: 17 g
cholesterolContent: 10 mg
fiberContent: 1 g
proteinContent: 6 g
saturatedFatContent: 3 g
sodiumContent: 173 mg
sugarContent: 1 g
fatContent: 8 g
unsaturatedFatContent: 0 g

Ingredients:

- 1.5 cups rotini pasta
- 3 tablespoons pesto, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 0.25 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 0.25 teaspoon granulated garlic
- 0.125 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 0.5 cup halved grape tomatoes
- 0.5 cup small (pearlini) fresh mozzarella balls
- 2 leaves fresh basil leaves, finely shredded

Instructions:

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook the rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; drain.
2. Mix pesto, olive oil, salt, granulated garlic, and black pepper in a bowl; add rotini. Toss to coat. Fold in tomatoes, mozzarella, and fresh basil.

2. Peach and Tomato Caprese Salad

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: minutes
Yield: 4 servings

Nutritional Facts:

calories: 254 kcal
carbohydrateContent: 8 g
cholesterolContent: 45 mg
fiberContent: 1 g
proteinContent: 11 g
saturatedFatContent: 9 g
sodiumContent: 569 mg
sugarContent: 7 g
fatContent: 19 g
unsaturatedFatContent: 0 g

Ingredients:

- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon flaked salt, divided
- 2 large heirloom tomatoes, thinly sliced
- 2 ripe peaches - halved, pitted, and sliced into half moons
- 1 (8 ounce) ball fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
- 6 leaves fresh basil

Instructions:

1. Whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and 1 pinch flaked salt in a small bowl until dressing is smooth.
2. Alternate tomato slices, peach slices, mozzarella slices, and basil leaves in layers on a serving platter. Drizzle dressing over salad and sprinkle remaining flaked salt on top.

calories: 202 kcal
carbohydrateContent: 3 g
cholesterolContent: 40 mg
fiberContent: 1 g
proteinContent: 9 g
saturatedFatContent: 9 g
sodiumContent: 143 mg
sugarContent: 2 g
fatContent: 16 g
unsaturatedFatContent: 0 g

Ingredients:

- 2 medium ripe tomatoes, sliced
- 2 (4 ounce) balls burrata cheese. sliced
- 10 leaves fresh basil
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, or more to taste

4. Arugula Caprese Salad

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: minutes
Yield: 6 servings

Nutritional Facts:

calories: 114 kcal
carbohydrateContent: 5 g
cholesterolContent: 16 mg
fiberContent: 1 g
proteinContent: 5 g
saturatedFatContent: 3 g
sodiumContent: 232 mg
sugarContent: 1 g
fatContent: 8 g
unsaturatedFatContent: 0 g

Ingredients:

- 1 (5 ounce) package baby arugula
- 1 cup halved grape tomatoes
- 1 cup small fresh mozzarella balls
- 0.5 cup garlic seasoned croutons
- 1 tablespoon basil paste
- 0.25 cup balsamic vinaigrette salad dressing
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Toss arugula, tomatoes, mozzarella cheese balls, and croutons in a large bowl.
2. Whisk basil paste into the balsamic salad dressing; pour on salad and toss to combine. Season salad with salt and black pepper, if desired.

5. Spinach Caprese Salad

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: minutes
Yield: 2 servings

Nutritional Facts:

calories: 415 kcal
carbohydrateContent: 9 g
cholesterolContent: 89 mg
fiberContent: 2 g
proteinContent: 22 g
saturatedFatContent: 17 g
sodiumContent: 183 mg
sugarContent: 7 g
fatContent: 31 g
unsaturatedFatContent: 0 g

Ingredients:

- 1 cup baby spinach leaves
- 1 large tomato, sliced 3/4 inch thick
- 1 ball of fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions:

1. Arrange spinach on a serving plate; top with tomato slices. Top each tomato slice with 1 slice fresh mozzarella. Sprinkle basil over salad; drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil.
